Purpose & Objectives

Objective Lesson Agenda Tomorrow's Extension
Students will be able to create text structures within their nonfiction writing in order to understand how parts of the text relate to the whole.

Question #2

Pizza is so old that its origins are not quite clear to historians, but the word pizza comes from the Latin verb pinsere, meaning to press. It may also be related to the Greek word pitta. In any event the word “pizza” was first documented in Italy in 997 AD. Whatever they were eating back then, however, was quite different from the pizza that we know today. Pizza as we know it could not really have been invented until sometime during the 16th century, when tomatoes were brought to Europe from America.
